Why this app..

→ This app is a management portal specific to Nano Escrow Service Providers.

→ To find out more see this Presentation


This works on all Android 4+ devices.

  1. On your Android device, open CROME browser
  2. Activate https://waynephilip.skunks.co as website
  3. Tap Add to home screen from the ≡ menu..
  4. Follow the instructions to install App
  5. CLOSE CHROME
  6. There should be an app icon on your phone desktop somewhere

This works on most Apple (iOS) devices..

  1. On your iPhone, open SAFARI browser
  2. Activate https://waynephilip.skunks.co as website
  3. Tap the “share”
  4. Tap Add to home screen option.
  5. Follow the instructions to install the App
  6. CLOSE SAFARI
  7. There should be an App icon on your iPhone desktop somewhere
